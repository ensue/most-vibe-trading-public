# Psychological Mechanism Map — Template

This file is YOUR psychological map. The AI builds it with you over time through structured investigation sessions.

---

## How to use this file

1. The AI will guide you through an investigation of **what actually drives your trading behavior** — not what you think drives it, but what the evidence shows.
2. Findings land here as they accumulate.
3. Once a finding recurs enough to count as operational truth, it gets promoted to `profile.md`.

## Sections to fill (with AI guidance)

### 1. The Core Engine
What is the chain that leads from ordinary life to opening the exchange? What does the market provide that ordinary life does not?

### 2. What Is Actually Missing
What needs are under-supplied in your life that the market substitutes for? (Common: stimulation, proof of worth, social connection, identity validation, boredom relief)

### 3. The Trading Sequence
What happens inside you from the moment you open the exchange to the moment the position is closed? Map the phases. Where does control degrade?

### 4. Named Traps
What are YOUR specific patterns? Name them. Give them trigger signs. Make them recognizable in real-time.

### 5. What Actually Works
What are the real, non-market sources of aliveness and meaning in your life? What has worked before to reduce compulsive behavior?

### 6. State Regulation Map
Which internal states are high-risk? Which are low-risk? What helps in each?

### 7. Key Formulations
Your own words, from your clearest moments. These are the sentences that cut through the noise when the noise returns.

---

## Why this matters for trading

The trading problem is usually not analytical. It is that the market is being used to regulate an internal state (emptiness, boredom, identity wound, stimulation deficit). As long as that is true, any real edge gets hijacked by the regulation function. This map exists to make the regulation visible — so the edge can operate cleanly.

## The Patterns

_AI populates this section as behavioral patterns emerge from trading conversations. Common destructive patterns to watch for:_

### Escalation
_Does session risk grow over time? Do position sizes increase within a single session?_

### Dopamine Trap
_Do you watch uPNL compulsively? Do profitable trades turn into losses because closing = ending the stimulation?_

### Plan Corruption
_Do plans get modified mid-trade? Do stops move? Do targets adjust under emotional load?_

### Serial Entry
_After a stop-out, do you immediately re-enter? Multiple trades on same pair after a loss?_

### Exhaustion Impairment
_Do long sessions degrade decision quality? At what hour mark?_

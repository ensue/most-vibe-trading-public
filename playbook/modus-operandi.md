# Personalized Modus Operandi — Template

This file is YOUR trading operating manual, designed around YOUR psychology. The AI helps you build it from the investigation in `synthesis/mechanism-map.md`.

---

## How to use this file

1. Complete the psychological investigation first (`synthesis/mechanism-map.md`).
2. Based on findings, the AI helps you design a trade structure, protocols, and trap countermeasures that fit YOUR specific failure modes.
3. This becomes the document you follow on every trade. Not generic rules — YOUR rules, designed for YOUR brain.

---

## Sections to build (with AI guidance)

### Ground Rules
Your non-negotiable rules, with the specific reason each one exists FOR YOU.

### Trade Structure
How many TPs? Runner or no runner? What split? The AI helps you choose based on your psychological profile (decision fatigue tolerance, post-partial behavior, context-switching patterns).

### Pre-Trade Protocol
Step-by-step checklist customized to your triggers. Includes state check, plan statement, verification, pre-mortem.

### Position Management
What decisions are pre-committed vs. real-time? Goal: minimize decisions during the trade to prevent decision fatigue and Phase 3 activation.

### Post-Trade Protocol
What to do after wins, losses, and liquidations. Includes specific countermeasures for YOUR post-trade traps (reversal urge, serial entry, etc.).

### Named Traps
YOUR patterns with recognition cards: trigger sign, what the brain says, what to do instead.

### Between Trades
What to do when flat and restless. Alternative activities that provide salience without market risk.

### Progression Path
Milestones, what changes at each stage, how to earn more freedom over time.

---

## The principle

> Making money on the exchange does not require better analysis. It requires the ability to return to the cold analytical state without needing to escalate along the way.

The modus operandi is the engineering solution to that problem: pre-commit every decision possible, minimize real-time choices, name every trap before it fires, and give the brain something to do between trades that isn't gambling.

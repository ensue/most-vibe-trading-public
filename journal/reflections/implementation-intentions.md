# Implementation Intentions

Pre-committed if-then plans. Written cold. Executed hot. Bypasses willpower.

Research: shifts goal attainment from ~22% to ~62% (Gollwitzer, 1999).

---

## Active Intentions

### After stop-out
**If** I get stopped out, **then** I will ___.

### Phone urge
**If** I see a setup on my phone, **then** I will ___.

### Re-entry urge
**If** I feel the urge to re-enter the same pair after a loss, **then** I will ___.

### Late night
**If** it is after ___ PM and I have an open position, **then** I will ___.

### First loss of session
**If** my first trade of the day is a loss, **then** I will ___.

### Payday protocol
**Trigger:** Payday or any day large income lands.
**If** I receive my paycheck, **then** BEFORE any exchange activity:
1. Pay all bills, rent, subscriptions, debts — obligations first.
2. Set aside living expenses for the month.
3. Wait minimum **48 hours** before any exchange deposit.
4. Trading bankroll is a **fixed budget**, not "whatever feels right."

### Loss acceptance before action
**If** I just took a loss and feel the urge to "fix it," **then:**
1. Say/type: "I lost $[X]. That was real money. It hurts."
2. No "but" — no reframe, no lesson. Sit with it for 60 seconds.
3. After 60 seconds: "The money is gone. I still have $[remaining]. What do I want to do?"
4. Only then consider next steps.

### Self-hatred circuit breaker
**If** I catch myself thinking "I'm pathetic / worthless," **then** type that thought in the workspace exactly as it appears and let the AI respond.

### Driving = no trading
**If** I am driving, the exchange app does NOT open. Period.

---

## How to use

- Fill these in during a calm session. Be specific — "close the laptop" not "take a break."
- AI will reference the specific intention when it detects the trigger.
- Update during calm/reflective sessions only. Never edit while activated.
- Add new intentions as new trigger patterns emerge.
